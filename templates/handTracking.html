Hand Tracking Module

The Hand Tracking module utilizes machine learning technologies to track human hand movements in real-time, creating a wide range of applications from gaming, sign language interpretation to human-computer interaction research.


How to use

  • Position your hand in front of the camera as shown in the video feed below. The module will automatically identify and track the hand landmarks.
  • Ensure your hand is fully visible to the camera and is not obscured by any objects. Also, make sure the lighting conditions are adequate.
